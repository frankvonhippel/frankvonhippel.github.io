Hello! Welcome to Frank von Hippel's website.

Dr. Frank Arthur von Hippel is a professor of ecotoxicology at Northern Arizona University, in Flagstaff, Arizona.

Podcast Publications @sci_history

Monthly interviews on important moments in the history of science. Hosted by Frank A. von Hippel, NAU ecotoxicology professor and author of The Chemical Age. Listeners in over 80 countries. Featured on Environmental Health News, the ACADAME BLOG, AZ Daily Sun, NAU News, NAU News again, NAZ Today, HEEDS, NAU Research, Conservation International, The Lowell Observer, the Alliance for Global Water Adaptation Facebook page, and the Society for Conservation Biology March 2020 newsletter.

Click on the title of any episode to listen to it on Libsyn.
